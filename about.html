About Us

At the Church of Christ Southwest Chicago, we are committed to following the teachings of the New Testament. We aim to worship God in spirit and truth, love one another sincerely, and share His Gospel with the world.

We are a diverse group of believers united by our faith in Jesus Christ, committed to studying the Bible, growing spiritually, and serving our community with compassion.

Whether you're new to the area or seeking a Christ-centered community, we invite you to worship, study, and fellowship with us.
